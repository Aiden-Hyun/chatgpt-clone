# MalloAI Account Deletion

**Last Updated:** December 2024

## How to Delete Your Account

1. Open the MalloAI app
2. Tap the **menu icon** (☰) to open the sidebar
3. Tap **Settings**
4. Scroll down to the **Data & Privacy** section
5. Tap **Delete Account**
6. Read the information about what will happen
7. Check the confirmation box that says "I understand my account will be deleted after 14 days unless I log back in"
8. Tap **Schedule Deletion**

## What Happens After You Request Deletion

### Immediate Effects
- You will be **logged out** of the app immediately
- Your deletion request will be recorded

### 14-Day Grace Period
- Your account is scheduled for permanent deletion **14 days** from your request
- During this period, you can **cancel the deletion** by simply logging back into the app
- If you log back in, the deletion request is automatically cancelled and your account remains active

### After 14 Days
- Your account will be **permanently deleted**
- This action is **irreversible**

## Data That Will Be Deleted

When your account is deleted, the following data will be permanently removed:

| Data Type | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| **Account Information** | Email address, display name, profile data |
| **Chat History** | All conversations and messages with AI |
| **Preferences** | Language settings, theme preferences |
| **Session Data** | Login tokens and authentication data |

## Data Retention

- No personal data is retained after account deletion
- Anonymized, aggregated usage statistics may be retained for service improvement
- Data shared with AI providers (OpenAI, Anthropic) during conversations is subject to their respective data retention policies

## How to Cancel a Pending Deletion

If you've requested account deletion but changed your mind:

1. Simply **log back into the app** within 14 days
2. Your deletion request will be **automatically cancelled**
3. Your account and all data will remain intact

Alternatively:
1. Open the app and log in
2. Go to **Settings** → **Delete Account**
3. You'll see your pending deletion date
4. Tap **Cancel Deletion Request**
